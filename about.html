ALL YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT



With the sun all set high in the sky and warm wind blazing in out of the window, VIKSPIK was born on 09 May, 2020. Having a keen interest in photography and great attraction of nature and man made architecture, I Vikas Verma, set out on a journey to capture the world's most amazing scenarios in my lens. The process goes on till date and by God's grace will continue forever. Capturing the eternal beauty and the unending attraction of beautiful scenes, be it natural or man made, mark the end of a beautiful day.

No matter up to what humanity advances, Nature always surpases it in every manner. Thus comparing the two is not a thing to be done. Asking about my major interests, surely, nature fantasizes me much more than the Man made architectures, yet this fact cannot be denied that Man's ability to build amazing sculptures and mind blowing architectures has been a pleasure to eyes.

Thus VIKSPIK aims to capture and present before the world the amazing scenarios and the beauty that Nature and Mankind together make in this amazing World.

So stay tuned, and do SUBSCRIBE us for some amazing views throught the lens.
